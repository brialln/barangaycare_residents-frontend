Program Overview

The Social Pension for Indigent Senior Citizens is a government program in the Philippines aimed at providing financial assistance to elderly individuals who are considered indigent. The program is managed by the Department of Social Welfare and Development (DSWD) and is designed to support the basic needs of senior citizens who do not receive any form of pension or regular income.
